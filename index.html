GestureFlow

Enterprise-grade hand gesture recognition. Real-time, accurate, and completely private. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Real-Time Detection
60+ FPS gesture recognition with sub-100ms latency. Optimized for smooth, responsive interactions across all devices.
ML-Powered
Advanced machine learning model trained on 1M+ hand gesture samples. Achieves 97%+ accuracy on common gestures.
Precision Tracking
21-point hand skeleton detection with sub-pixel accuracy. Capture every nuance of hand movement and orientation.
Multi-Hand Support
Simultaneously detect and differentiate between multiple hands. Perfect for collaborative and interactive experiences.
Privacy First
100% local processing. Zero data transmission, zero tracking, complete privacy. Your hand data never leaves your device.
Developer Friendly
Simple, intuitive API. Integrate gesture recognition into any web application in minutes with minimal code.

Recognized Gestures

✊ Fist
All fingers closed and contracted. Perfect for confirmation or power gestures.
✋ Open Palm
All five fingers fully extended. Universal stop or open gesture.
✌ Victory
Index and middle fingers extended upward. Classic peace or victory sign.
👍 Thumbs Up
Thumb extended, other fingers closed. Universal approval gesture.
☝ Pointing
Index finger extended, others closed. Use for targeting or selection.
👌 OK Sign
Thumb and index forming a circle. Indicates approval or understanding.
