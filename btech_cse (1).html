// 01 — COURSE STRUCTURE

4-YEAR ROADMAP

YEAR 1 — FOUNDATION
Building the Base
Mathematics (Calculus, Linear Algebra, Discrete Math), Physics, Introduction to Programming (C/C++), Basic Electronics, Engineering Drawing, and Communication Skills. This year lays the logical and mathematical foundation for everything ahead.
YEAR 2 — CORE CS
Diving Into Computer Science
Data Structures & Algorithms, Object-Oriented Programming (Java/C++), Digital Logic & Computer Organisation, Probability & Statistics, Database Management Systems, and Operating Systems fundamentals. This is where real CS thinking begins.
YEAR 3 — DEPTH + SPECIALISATION
Going Deeper
Computer Networks, Theory of Computation, Compiler Design, Software Engineering, Electives begin (AI/ML, Web Dev, Cybersecurity, etc.), and your first internship. This year defines your technical identity and area of interest.
YEAR 4 — PROJECT + PLACEMENT
Capstone & Launch
Major Project / Thesis, advanced electives, internship conversions, campus placements, and preparation for higher education (GATE, GRE, CAT, UPSC). This year is where it all comes together — from student to professional.
// 02 — ACADEMICS

CORE SUBJECTS

🧮
Data Structures & Algorithms

The heart of CS. Arrays, linked lists, trees, graphs, sorting, searching, dynamic programming, and algorithm complexity. Mastery here is the gateway to top tech companies and competitive programming.

Key Topics
Time ComplexityGraph AlgorithmsDynamic ProgrammingRecursionHashing
🖥️
Operating Systems

How computers manage resources. Process management, scheduling, memory management, file systems, deadlocks, and concurrency. Essential for systems programming, backend roles, and kernel development.

Key Topics
Process SchedulingMemory ManagementConcurrencyFile SystemsDeadlocks
🗄️
Database Management Systems

Relational databases, SQL, normalisation, transactions, indexing, and NoSQL concepts. Every modern application relies on databases — this subject is universally applicable across all software roles.

Key Topics
SQLNormalisationTransactions & ACIDIndexingNoSQL Basics
🌐
Computer Networks

How the internet works — TCP/IP stack, HTTP, DNS, routing, subnetting, and network security. Critical for cloud computing, cybersecurity, DevOps, and backend engineering roles.

Key Topics
TCP/IP StackRouting ProtocolsDNS & HTTPSubnettingNetwork Security
🧠
Artificial Intelligence

Search algorithms, knowledge representation, expert systems, planning, and introduction to machine learning. This core subject opens the door to the field transforming every industry in India and globally.

Key Topics
Search AlgorithmsHeuristicsKnowledge RepresentationIntro to ML
📐
Theory of Computation

The mathematical foundations of CS — automata, formal languages, Turing machines, and computational complexity (P vs NP). Builds rigorous thinking about what computers can and cannot solve.

Key Topics
Finite AutomataContext-Free GrammarsTuring MachinesComplexity Classes
🔧
Software Engineering

Building software that works at scale. SDLC models, Agile/Scrum, design patterns, UML, testing strategies, and project management. Bridges coding and real-world product development.

Key Topics
Agile / ScrumDesign PatternsTestingUMLVersion Control
⚙️
Computer Organisation & Architecture

How CPUs work — instruction sets, pipelining, memory hierarchy, cache design, and parallel processing. Understanding hardware-software interaction makes you a better systems programmer.

Key Topics
CPU DesignPipeliningCache & Memory HierarchyInstruction Sets

// 04 — CAREER PATHS

WHERE YOU WORK

💼
Software Development Engineer

The most common CSE career. Build products at companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Flipkart, or hundreds of Indian startups. Roles span frontend, backend, full-stack, mobile, and systems engineering.

Avg Starting Salary (India)
₹6–15 LPA (Tier 3) · ₹15–30 LPA (NIT) · ₹30–60+ LPA (IIT/IIIT)
📈
Data Scientist / ML Engineer

Build predictive models, design ML pipelines, and extract business insights from data. One of the fastest-growing and highest-paying roles in India's tech ecosystem — especially in fintech, e-commerce, and healthtech.

Avg Starting Salary (India)
₹10–25 LPA · Research roles up to ₹40+ LPA
🔒
Cybersecurity Analyst / Ethical Hacker

Protect systems from threats. Roles include Pen Tester, SOC Analyst, Security Engineer, and Forensic Investigator. Government, defence, banking, and tech all hire aggressively — supply is far below demand.

Avg Starting Salary (India)
₹6–18 LPA · Specialists earn ₹25–50 LPA
☁️
Cloud / DevOps Engineer

Design and maintain cloud infrastructure, automate deployments, and ensure system reliability at scale. Cloud engineers with AWS/Azure certifications are among India's most in-demand tech professionals right now.

Avg Starting Salary (India)
₹8–20 LPA · Senior roles ₹30–60 LPA
📦
Product Manager

Lead the vision, strategy, and roadmap of tech products. CSE background gives you technical credibility non-technical PMs lack. Usually entered after 2–4 years of engineering experience, or directly via MBA.

Avg Starting Salary (India)
₹15–40 LPA (APM roles at top product companies)
🏛️
Government / PSU / Research

ISRO, DRDO, C-DAC, ECIL, BSNL, and other organisations hire CSE graduates via GATE. Offers stability, national service, and exciting research in defence, space, and national digital infrastructure.

Selection Route
GATE Score → PSU Direct Recruitment
// 05 — WHAT COMES NEXT

AFTER B.TECH

🎓
M.Tech in India
Pursue M.Tech at IITs, NITs, or IIITs via GATE score. Specialise in AI/ML, Systems, Networks, or VLSI. Often comes with a GATE scholarship (₹12,400/month). Opens doors to academia, research, and senior tech roles at premium packages.
✈️
MS / PhD Abroad
Apply to universities in the USA (Stanford, MIT, CMU, UIUC), Canada, Germany, or UK via GRE + TOEFL. Top MS programmes offer Teaching/Research Assistantships covering tuition + living costs. Leads to global tech careers and research positions.
💼
MBA (IIM & Top B-Schools)
CSE graduates are among the most preferred MBA applicants. Crack CAT for IIMs or GMAT for ISB/global MBAs. Opens paths to consulting (McKinsey, BCG), investment banking, and tech product management at senior levels.
🚀
Entrepreneurship / Startup
Build your own startup. India's startup ecosystem is the 3rd largest globally — incubators at IITs and NITs offer funding and mentorship. Many Indian unicorns (Flipkart, Zomato, CRED) were founded by CSE graduates.
📜
GATE → PSU / Research Labs
GATE opens doors to M.Tech at IITs and direct PSU recruitment at ISRO, DRDO, ECIL, BSNL, and more. Government roles offer exceptional job security, research opportunities, and deep national impact.
🏛️
Civil Services (IAS / IES)
CSE engineers can appear for UPSC Civil Services (IAS/IPS/IFS) or the Indian Engineering Services (IES) exam, which places selected candidates into technical government departments at Group A officer level.
📱
Freelancing & Remote Work
Build a global client base on Upwork, Toptal, or Fiverr. Senior CSE freelancers earn ₹3,000–₹10,000/hour for specialised work. Remote jobs at US/EU companies via platforms like Turing offer global salaries from India.
📚
Teaching & Academia
Pursue M.Tech or PhD and join academia as an Assistant Professor. AICTE-approved colleges require NET/SET alongside postgraduate qualifications. IITs and NITs require PhD + strong research publication record.
🎖️
Defence / Armed Forces (Technical)
Join the Indian Army, Navy, or Air Force in technical officer roles via UPSC CDS or Short Service Commission. Technical branches leverage your CSE background in signals intelligence and cyber warfare units.
